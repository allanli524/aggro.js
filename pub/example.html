This could be your favourite anime viewing site...

Go ahead and explore, try to hover over and click on the thumbnails.

Instructions

  • Hovering over a thumbnail: you will see a pop up table that shows the target series's information.
  • Unhovering a thumbnail: the pop up information table will disappear.
  • Clicking a thumbnail: the information table will lock in place, showing a "more information" button which you can click to access the plot synopsis and link to a trailer video.
  • Clicking multiple thumbnails: the information table of the most recent thumbnail will show up on top of others, by clicking between different thumbnails, you can compare different shows.
  • Clicking the unlock button: the information pop up will be unlocked, now it will disappear once you unhover.
